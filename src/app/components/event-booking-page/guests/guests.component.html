• Aish Kharbanda

    Aish Kharbanda is a celebrated Instagram influencer and YouTube content creator known for his engaging anime-related content. With a passion for Japanese animation and pop culture, Aish has garnered a dedicated following, sharing insights, reviews, and discussions that resonate with anime enthusiasts worldwide.

  • AnimeTM Talks

    AnimeTM Talks, helmed by Tarun Saini, is a prominent voice in the Indian anime community, offering a rich blend of anime discussions, reviews, and insights. With a strong presence on YouTube and Instagram, AnimeTM Talks engages fans with diverse content, from top anime lists to in-depth cultural explorations. His CHannel had a profound impact by creating the renowned Hindi dub of the anime movie "YourName" in 2018.
